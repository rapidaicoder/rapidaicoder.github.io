# Content & Tone
- **Tone of Voice:** Technical and Authoritative. The language should be precise and efficient, using developer-centric terminology to establish trust and expertise.
- **Language:** Bilingual (Simplified Chinese and English) across all pages to cater to a global developer audience.

# Visual Identity
- **Primary Theme:** Modern Blue. A professional palette of deep and vibrant blues to evoke stability, innovation, and a high-tech feel.
- **Backgrounds:** Use of abstract tech patterns, such as subtle geometric grids or network-inspired motifs, to create depth and a futuristic atmosphere.
- **Media:** Integration of high-quality UI screenshots and animated GIFs showcasing the AICoder CLI in action.

# User Experience & Layout
- **Site Structure:** Multi-page architecture including dedicated sections for Home, Features, Download, Tutorials, and Support.
- **Navigation:** A sticky top navigation bar (Navbar) that remains visible on scroll, ensuring persistent access to all site sections.
- **Call-to-Action (CTA):** The primary CTA "Install AICoder Now" should be prominently featured in the hero section and at key transition points.

# Brand Messaging
- **Focus:** AICoder is the essential bridge to the "vibe coding" era, transforming complex tool configurations into a seamless experience.
